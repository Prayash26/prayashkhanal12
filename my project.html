Tourist Attractions

Sai Mandir

Daramdin Sai Temple is a religious centre and a significant place of worship for the Sai devotees, located at Daramdin Village in West Sikkim. The foundation stone for the temple was laid in the month of December 2012 by the Chief Minister of Sikkim Sri Pawan Chamling. After the completion of the construction, Sai Mandir Daramdin was inaugurated on 15th April 2007..

Varsey Rhododendron Santury

The Varsey Rhododendron Sanctuary or Barsey Rhododendron Sanctuary occupies 104 km2 in the Singalila Range in western Sikkim. It borders on Nepal to the west, and on the state of West Bengal to the south across the Rambong Khola stream. The rhododendrons bloom during March till May..

About Our Village Sombaria west sikkim

Sombaria is a serene village located in West Sikkim, nestled amidst lush green hills and picturesque landscapes. It is known for its pleasant weather, warm hospitality, and vibrant culture. The village offers stunning views of the Kanchenjunga range and is surrounded by tea gardens, cardamom fields, and rich biodiversity. A peaceful destination, Sombaria serves as a gateway to lesser-explored trekking trails and is home to a blend of traditional Sikkimese and Nepali communities, making it a unique cultural hotspot.
